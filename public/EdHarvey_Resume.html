Data Science Expertise

My expertise in data science originates from multivariate statistics that bridge the spatial scales of ecology and biogeography. Field experiments are combined with large databases of species distribution, multispectral satellite imagery, climate, and GIS land use. I employed classification and regression tree methods, K-means clustering, principal components analysis, generalized additive and linear models, and spatial and geostatistics. I also applied data analytics to the IT industry, most notably a performance analysis of S2S message processing that ran daily on 60,000 messages, and a 1-year analysis of 6.3 million messages. This was a significant effort with short deadlines requiring exploratory analysis of performance trends by message type and time of day/week, and interactive visualizations.
